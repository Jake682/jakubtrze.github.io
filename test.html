"What got you into podcasts?"
I've always really enjoyed listening to people talking about what they're passionate about.

"Why did you start this?"
I noticed a significant gap in the market for podcast editing services.
After conducting extensive research, I found that there were only two options available:

Hiring a Fiverr/Upwork freelancer for $20-40 or
a professional for a fixed price of $200-400/hour-long episode taking them 5-10 hours to edit.

I offer customized editing solutions to fit your podcast and budget needs.
My goal is to create unmatched value for podcasters.
I've made this graph on the right to help illustrate this point.

The Basic and Standard Packages provide the highest jump in quality
and I recommend starting there.

I'm happy to provide custom editing for above $120 to anyone interested, just keep in mind
that  the improvement in quality will be less visible compared to previous packages.
