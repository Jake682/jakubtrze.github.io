PROCESSING & MASTERING The Processing and Mastering Package is an ideal option for new podcasters or those seeking a budget-friendly solution. This package provides essential podcast editing services to guarantee that your episodes sound refined and professional. You can concentrate solely on creating captivating content, which I will transform into upload-ready podcast episodes. Processing and Mastering Package Key Features:
Adding Intro, Outro, Midroll
Putting all of the episode's components together, seamlessly fading in pre-recorded fragments like intro, outro, midroll and music if provided. 
The sound processing feature is designed to elevate the quality and clarity of your podcast's audio. It comprises two crucial elements: Equalization (EQ) and Compression.
With equalization, I can fine-tune your podcast's audio, making it more enjoyable for your listeners. It's like giving your podcast a little audio makeover to ensure that every word sounds clear and pleasing to the ear.
The compression feature ensures a balanced and consistent sound throughout your podcast. It smooths out volume fluctuations, providing a better listening experience for your audience. Say goodbye to sudden loud peaks and soft whispers—compression keeps your audio levels steady and engaging.
Basic Noise Reduction
A lot of the unwanted low-frequency noise is gone after the EQ, for the quiet background I use a carefully adjusted noise gate, making sure it doesn't chop the beginnings or ends of any words.
If after that there's still unwanted noise I will use the Izotope RX denoising tool to reduce it, it's pretty good however it always affects audio quality a bit - it removes all the frequencies that have the unwanted noise in them
so the final recording has gaps in it. I prefer to think about what's causing this noise in the first place and fix that.
1 Editing Pass over the first 5 minutes
The beginning of an episode is the most important, that's why I want to focus on making it as clear and engaging as possible.  I can also remove any other fragments if you provide the timestamps for them. 
Basic Volume Leveling
Adjusting episode volume to have an average of -16 for stereo and -19 for mono(the standard), as well as making sure that after those changes there are no peaks.
48 hours turnaround time
It's easiest for me if we stick to a regular schedule. I can then reserve specific time for the editing of your podcast.
One Revision
For the 10 minutes sample I take as much feedback and suggestions as you can give me, to ensure that we are on the same page. After that, there's one more extra revision for the first episode.

Audio Enhancement: I can help improve the sound quality of recordings that have challenging audio conditions.

Advanced Noise Reduction
After listening for unwanted noise on different pairs of studio monitors and headphones, I can more accurately remove noise from the recording. Again, there is a compromise - the more noise gets removed, the more important frequencies also get removed from the track.

throughout the entire episode.
